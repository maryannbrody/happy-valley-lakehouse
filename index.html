Heating & Cooling

Main Room

The split unit is controlled by a thermostat on the windowsill. It handles both heat and AC.

Bathroom

There's a baseboard heater with a knob — turn it as needed.

Bunk & Guest Rooms

The wall thermostat is behind the bunk room door. Use that for both rooms. Please don't touch the baseboard thermostats — they're set where they need to be.

Wood Stove

There's a wood stove in the main room, and using it is one of the best parts of staying here. Firewood is stocked outside. Open the damper before lighting, start with kindling and small pieces, and let it build. Close the damper partway once the fire is established to control the burn. Make sure the fire is fully out before leaving the house or going to bed.

Kitchen

We keep the kitchen stocked with basics — olive oil, salt, pepper, spices, coffee, tea, and a few other staples. Help yourself to anything that's here. If you use something up, no worries at all.

Trash & Recycling

Shutesbury requires yellow town trash bags — you'll find them under the sink. The trash barrel is under the deck. For recycling, please separate paper from plastics and containers. Recycling goes in the bins next to the barrel.
